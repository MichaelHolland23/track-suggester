Which track are you best suited for?

The world of web design offers many possible routes for your career. One of the most important decisions you will make before you dive in, is about the programming language you will choose to focus on. Fortunately, Epicodus has you covered!

Here are some of the things to consider before choosing your track:

  • Do you want to work for a large, established company or a small, hip start-up?
  • Are you more interested in designing cool-looking websites or do you want to learn more about how websites work?
  • Are you interested in developing apps or general internet sofware?
